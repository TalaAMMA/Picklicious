After living abroad for more than 25 years and being exposed to many international cultures and cuisines. We decided to move back home and start our own family business. We were determined to come up with an innovative concept that combines our love for food and the best homemade gourmet delicious pickles.

“Picklicious” was born, to offer a variety of scrumptious and yummy-luscious products, as well as an assortment of sauces and chutneys. Picklicious also offers one of the highest quality infused salts and fleur de sel made of 100% natural, unrefined, unpolluted, with no preservatives added, produced in one of Lebanon's most ancient and renowned towns Anfeh, where we can find the most beautiful and cleanest shorelines in Lebanon.

Our Mission

Our mission is to emphasize and encourage small local producers from regions all over Lebanon to collaborate with us in order to produce the best quality homemade products and goods to provide to our customers . Our mission is to also enlighten our customers about local Lebanese producers which we collaborate with to ensure the best products and goods are being produced, in order to provide the most suitable and authentic homemade products such as: salts, pickles, sauces, chutneys and much more!

Our Vision

Our vision is to produce the best quality of homemade products by using authentic Lebanese products from regions all over Lebanon, to be able to provide our customers with the highest quality of goods. Our vision is to enhance and educate the palates of our customers by serving exquisite and authentic homemade products, with the freshest ingredients and flavors available. Our vision is to attempt and make our homemade products available for customers all over Lebanon and to be able to expand our business worldwide.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make an effort to provide our homemade products and goods available for customers all over Lebanon, and strive to enter the international market in order to showcase the best Lebanese homemade products and goods.

Our Objective

Our objective is to provide our customers with the best quality homemade products and goods, which are produced from local Lebanese ingredients obtained from regions all over Lebanon, to showcase and highlight local business producers and their products.
